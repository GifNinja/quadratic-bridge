Dimensions

Highlighted = Useful information for the purpose of this project
  • Total bridge length: 1,260 feet (387m)
  • Bridge width: 18 feet (5m)
  • Main span: 880 feet (268m)
  • Tower height: 150 feet (46m)
  • Deck Height: 956 feet above the Arkansas River
  • Walkway: 1,292 wooden planks, 250 of which needs to be replaced annually
  • Elevation: 6,700 feet above sea level
  • 2,100 strands of No. 9 galvanised wire in the cables, each weighing 300 tons
  • 1000 tons of steel on the bridge floor
  • Supports in excess of two million pounds of mass
